NLP & Text Mining Innovations

Natural Language Processing (NLP) and text mining are revolutionizing the way we extract insights from unstructured data. By leveraging AI models like BERT and GPT, we can analyze vast text corpora, uncover patterns, and automate decision-making. These innovations power applications such as intelligent chatbots, sentiment analysis, topic modeling, and information retrieval—transforming industries like healthcare, finance, and customer experience. Here is my contribution to advancing NLP technology—through projects that explore AI-powered accident cause identification, sentiment analysis, and text classification. Each project reflects my commitment to harnessing NLP for real-world impact and pushing the boundaries of what machines can understand from human language.
